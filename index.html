Our Projects

FarmFlock

An IoT-based system that helps farmers monitor feeding schedules and vaccination programs for their poultry. Automated SMS alerts ensure timely reminders, reduce manual errors, and improve overall flock health and productivity. The system also allows tracking of feed consumption, vaccination history, and growth patterns for smarter farm management.
Water Usage Monitoring & Billing

An IoT-based smart metering system that monitors water and energy consumption in real-time. It provides automated billing, usage analytics, and alerts for overconsumption. The system helps users and institutions track usage patterns, reduce waste, and optimize resource management efficiently.
Hazardous Gas Detection

An IoT-based system for early detection of hazardous gases such as carbon monoxide and methane. The system provides real-time alerts, visual dashboards, and automated notifications to prevent accidents and ensure safety. It is ideal for industrial, municipal, and environmental monitoring applications.
Roboken Competitions

Roboken competitions challenge innovators to design and build intelligent robotic systems. Participants showcase creativity, problem-solving, and technical skills by tackling real-world scenarios, from automation to environmental monitoring.
